iB Cricket announces world's first
Virtual Reality Cricket League


iB Cricket and Vitu together have announced the world's first virtual reality cricket league feauturing 12 International cricketers, who will be competing against each other for the iB cricket super over league title in Mumbai.Iconic cricketers like Virender Sehwag, suresh Raina, Brendon McCullum will be competing in the league.
