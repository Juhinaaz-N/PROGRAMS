BADHUSHA

Balushahi is a traditional dessert originating from the Indian subcontinent. It is similar to a glazed doughnut in terms of ingredients, but differs in texture and taste. In South India, a similar pastry is known as badushah.

SOAN PAPDI

Soan papdi is a popular dessert in the Indian subcontinent.It is usually cube-shaped or served as flakes and has a crispy & flaky texture.

DAIRY MILK

It is a British brand of milk chocolate manufactured by Cadbury. It was introduced in the United Kingdom in 1905 and now consists of a number of products. Every product in the Dairy Milk line is made with exclusively milk chocolate.

SNICKERS

It is a chocolate bar made by the American company Mars, Incorporated, consisting of nougat topped with caramel and peanuts that is encased in milk chocolate.
