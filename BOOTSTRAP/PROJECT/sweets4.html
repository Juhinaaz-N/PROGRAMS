SWEETS, The taste of sugar is a highly desired taste around the world and sharing sweets among one another signifies happiness and celebration. CHOCOLATES, The food product made from cocoa beans, consumed as candy and used to make beverages and to flavour or coat various confections and bakery products. Rich in carbohydrates, it is an excellent source of quick energy, and it also contains minute amounts of the stimulating alkaloids theobromine and caffeine.
