THANJAVUR

Thanjavur is the 11th biggest city in Tamil Nadu. Thanjavur is an important center of South Indian religion, art, and architecture.Thanjavur, a city in Tamil Nadu's central region, is known for its culture and architecture. The Ministry of Urban Development shortlisted the city as one of India's 100 smart cities.
