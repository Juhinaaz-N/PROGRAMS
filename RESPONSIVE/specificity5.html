Ferrero Rocher was born in 1982 after a very long process of research and development for excellence. Thanks to its unique and exquisite recipe, the refined golden wrapper and a distinctive transparent box, Ferrero Rocher was recognized since its launch as a true symbol of Ferrero quality and excellence.

The brand's focus on quality, its beautiful packaging of golden wrapper and generous amounts of hazelnut has made Ferrero Rocher one of the most loved and appreciated chocolate all over the world.

The chocolate consists of a whole roasted hazelnut encased in a thin wafer shell filled with hazelnut chocolate and covered in milk chocolate and chopped hazelnuts.
